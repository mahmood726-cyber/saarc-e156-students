Why It Matters

The Evidence   128 words · target 156
How does recruitment rate analysis inform the methodological quality of South Asian trials, given that saarc's large populations make it attractive for rapid recruitment? This cross-sectional audit examined 11,116 interventional trials across 8 SAARC nations on ClinicalTrials.gov. The primary estimand was the linear regression slope, computed from registry-level metadata. Analysis of recruitment rate analysis across 11,116 SAARC trials revealed India hosting 49% of the regional portfolio. Sensitivity analyses using alternative inequality metrics confirmed the primary finding with consistent direction and magnitude. This analysis is constrained by missing sub-national data and the exclusion of observational studies from the registry.
