The Evidence   125 words · target 156
What does drap approval efficiency reveal about research sovereignty in South Asia, given that drap's trial approval timeline has improved but still averages 6-12 months? This cross-sectional audit examined 4,669 interventional trials across 1 SAARC nations on ClinicalTrials.gov. The primary estimand was the Kaplan-Meier cumulative registration curve, computed from registry-level metadata. Analysis of drap approval efficiency across 4,669 SAARC trials revealed Pakistan hosting 100% of the regional portfolio. Sensitivity analyses using alternative inequality metrics confirmed the primary finding with consistent direction and magnitude. These governance patterns reveal that compared to 30-60 days for india's cdsco, creating a regulatory speed disadvantage that diverts trials from pakistan, threatening research sovereignty. This analysis is limited by single-registry scope and the absence of national trial databases from SAARC nations.
