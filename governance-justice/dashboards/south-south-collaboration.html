What does south-south collaboration reveal about research sovereignty in South Asia, given that south-south collaboration between saarc and african or latin american nations could create alternative research partnerships? This cross-sectional audit examined 11,116 interventional trials across 8 SAARC nations on ClinicalTrials.gov. The primary estimand was the network degree centrality, computed from registry-level metadata. Shannon entropy for south-south collaboration was 1.48 bits across 11,116 trials, indicating low distributional evenness. Shannon entropy of 1.48 bits corroborated the concentration finding, well below the maximum-evenness benchmark.
