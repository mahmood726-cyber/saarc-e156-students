What does whistleblower & misconduct reports reveal about research sovereignty in South Asia, given that research misconduct reporting in saarc is hampered by weak whistleblower protections? This cross-sectional audit examined 11,116 interventional trials across 8 SAARC nations on ClinicalTrials.gov. The primary estimand was the Poisson incidence rate, computed from registry-level metadata. Analysis of whistleblower & misconduct reports across 11,116 SAARC trials revealed India hosting 49% of the regional portfolio. Bayesian rate estimation (posterior mean 0.0222) confirmed the direction and magnitude of the primary finding.
