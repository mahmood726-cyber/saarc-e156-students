What does cro outsourcing patterns reveal about research sovereignty in South Asia, given that india is the world's largest cro outsourcing destination? This cross-sectional audit examined 11,116 interventional trials across 8 SAARC nations on ClinicalTrials.gov. The primary estimand was the Herfindahl-Hirschman concentration index, computed from registry-level metadata. The cro outsourcing patterns analysis yielded a Gini of 0.71, with India hosting 49% of 11,116 trials.
