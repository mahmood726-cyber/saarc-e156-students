Foreign Sponsor Dominance

What does foreign sponsor dominance reveal about research sovereignty in South Asia, given that foreign pharmaceutical companies sponsor the majority of saarc trials? This cross-sectional audit examined 11,116 interventional trials across 8 SAARC nations on ClinicalTrials.gov.
