The Evidence   119 words · target 156
Does Pakistan's Polio trial portfolio match the country's disease burden, given that pakistan remains one of two polio-endemic countries? We audited 12 Pakistan-based Polio trials registered on ClinicalTrials.gov through 2025. The primary estimand was the Kaplan-Meier cumulative registration curve, computed from registry-level metadata. Pakistan registered 12 trials with the distribution across the polio eradication last mile dimension showing marked inequality. Bayesian rate estimation (posterior mean 0.0000) confirmed the direction and magnitude of the primary finding. These findings confirm that with transmission persisting in southern kpk and balochistan — areas where operational research trials face security and access challenges, demanding targeted policy intervention. This analysis is limited by reliance on ClinicalTrials.gov alone, which may undercount locally registered South Asian studies.
