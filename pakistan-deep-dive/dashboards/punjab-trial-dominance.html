The Evidence   120 words · target 156
What does analysis of punjab trial dominance reveal about Pakistan's research landscape, given that punjab province hosts over 60% of pakistan's clinical trials? We audited 4,669 Pakistan-based interventional trials registered on ClinicalTrials.gov through 2025. The primary estimand was the Herfindahl-Hirschman concentration index, computed from registry-level metadata. Pakistan's 4,669 trials showed a Gini of 0.00, confirming severe within-country concentration that punjab trial dominance quantifies. The HHI of 10,000 independently corroborated the concentration finding across the regional trial landscape. These findings confirm that with lahore alone accounting for a disproportionate share, reflecting the province's medical infrastructure advantage and 110 million population, demanding targeted policy intervention. This analysis is constrained by missing sub-national data and the exclusion of observational studies from the registry.
