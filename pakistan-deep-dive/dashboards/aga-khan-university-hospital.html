The Evidence   124 words · target 156
What does analysis of aga khan university hospital reveal about Pakistan's research landscape, given that akuh anchors pakistan's clinical trial infrastructure with international accreditation and research networks? We audited 4,669 Pakistan-based interventional trials registered on ClinicalTrials.gov through 2025. The primary estimand was the Herfindahl-Hirschman concentration index, computed from registry-level metadata. Across Pakistan's 4,669 trials the HHI was 10,000, indicating high institutional concentration in the aga khan university hospital analysis. The HHI of 10,000 independently corroborated the concentration finding across the regional trial landscape. These findings confirm that but its private status means trial access is limited to patients who can afford its fees, demanding targeted policy intervention. This analysis is constrained by missing sub-national data and the exclusion of observational studies from the registry.
