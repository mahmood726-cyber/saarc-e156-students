Why It Matters

Shaukat Khanum Memorial Cancer Hospital dominates Pakistan's oncology trial landscape, but with only two campuses (Lahore, Peshawar), cancer trial access remains geographically restricted.

The Evidence   119 words · target 156
Does Pakistan's Cancer trial portfolio match the country's disease burden, given that shaukat khanum memorial cancer hospital dominates pakistan's oncology trial landscape? We audited 141 Pakistan-based Cancer trials registered on ClinicalTrials.gov through 2025. The primary estimand was the rate ratio comparing per-capita trial density, computed from registry-level metadata. Across Pakistan's 141 trials the HHI was 10,000, indicating high institutional concentration in the shaukat khanum cancer network analysis. The HHI of 10,000 independently corroborated the concentration finding across the regional trial landscape. These findings confirm that but with only two campuses (lahore, peshawar), cancer trial access remains geographically restricted, demanding targeted policy intervention. This analysis is constrained by missing sub-national data and the exclusion of observational studies from the registry.
