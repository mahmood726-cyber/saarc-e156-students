Why It Matters

The Evidence   150 words · target 156
Does Pakistan's Diabetes trial portfolio match the country's disease burden, given that pakistan has 33 million diabetes patients — the third highest globally — yet diabetes trial investment per patient is less than 1% of what european nations invest per diabetic person.? We audited 191 Pakistan-based Diabetes trials registered on ClinicalTrials.gov through 2025. The primary estimand was the linear regression slope, computed from registry-level metadata. Pakistan registered 191 trials with the distribution across the diabetes tsunami dimension showing marked inequality. Sensitivity analyses using alternative inequality metrics confirmed the primary finding with consistent direction and magnitude. These findings confirm that pakistan has 33 million diabetes patients — the third highest globally — yet diabetes trial investment per patient is less than 1% of what european nations invest per diabetic person, demanding targeted policy intervention. This analysis is limited by reliance on ClinicalTrials.gov alone, which may undercount locally registered South Asian studies.
