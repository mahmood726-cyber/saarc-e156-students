The Evidence   122 words · target 156
What does analysis of generic drug trial pipeline reveal about Pakistan's research landscape, given that pakistan's generic pharmaceutical industry could serve as a trial platform for affordable medicine development? We audited 4,669 Pakistan-based interventional trials registered on ClinicalTrials.gov through 2025. The primary estimand was the rate ratio comparing per-capita trial density, computed from registry-level metadata. Pakistan registered 4,669 trials with the distribution across the generic drug trial pipeline dimension showing marked inequality. Bayesian rate estimation (posterior mean 0.0093) confirmed the direction and magnitude of the primary finding. These findings confirm that but bioequivalence study quality varies and international recognition remains limited, demanding targeted policy intervention. This analysis is limited by reliance on ClinicalTrials.gov alone, which may undercount locally registered South Asian studies.
