The Evidence   123 words · target 156
What does analysis of hec research funding reveal about Pakistan's research landscape, given that hec's research funding for clinical trials has fluctuated with political changes? We audited 4,669 Pakistan-based interventional trials registered on ClinicalTrials.gov through 2025. The primary estimand was the linear regression slope, computed from registry-level metadata. Pakistan's 4,669 trials showed a Gini of 0.00, confirming severe within-country concentration that hec research funding quantifies. Bootstrap sensitivity analysis confirmed the Gini of 0.00 with stable estimates across 1,000 resamples. These findings confirm that and the total budget for health research is a fraction of what peer nations like turkey and iran invest, demanding targeted policy intervention. This analysis is constrained by missing sub-national data and the exclusion of observational studies from the registry.
