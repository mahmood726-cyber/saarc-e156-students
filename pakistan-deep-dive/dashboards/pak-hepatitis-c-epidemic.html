Why It Matters

Pakistan's hepatitis C prevalence of 5-8% is among the world's highest, driven by unsafe injection practices, yet the country is reliant on generic DAA trials designed elsewhere.

What does Pakistan's clinical trial landscape reveal about research capacity and equity, and does the distribution of Hepatitis C trials reveal a systematic research gap? This cross-sectional audit evaluated 15 South Asian trials registered on ClinicalTrials.gov across 1 SAARC nations.
