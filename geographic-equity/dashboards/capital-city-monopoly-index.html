How does capital city monopoly index expose spatial inequality in South Asian clinical research, given that capital cities monopolize saarc clinical trial infrastructure because they host major teaching hospitals? This cross-sectional audit examined 11,116 interventional trials across 8 SAARC nations on ClinicalTrials.gov. The primary estimand was the Herfindahl-Hirschman concentration index, computed from registry-level metadata. The capital city monopoly index analysis yielded a Gini of 0.71, with India hosting 49% of 11,116 trials.
