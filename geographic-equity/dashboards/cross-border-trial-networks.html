How does cross-border trial networks expose spatial inequality in South Asian clinical research, given that cross-border trial networks in saarc are rare despite shared languages? This cross-sectional audit examined 11,116 interventional trials across 8 SAARC nations on ClinicalTrials.gov. The primary estimand was the network degree centrality, computed from registry-level metadata. The cross-border trial networks HHI was 4,164.2, exceeding the concentration threshold, with India dominating at 49%. Shannon entropy of 1.48 bits corroborated the concentration finding, well below the maximum-evenness benchmark.
