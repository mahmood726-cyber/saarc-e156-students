E156 Format Rules (click to expand)
  1. Exactly 7 sentences (S1-S7), at most 156 words, single paragraph.
  2. No citations, links, or metadata inside the body text.
  3. S1 = Question (population, intervention, endpoint).
  4. S2 = Dataset (studies, participants, scope).
  5. S3 = Method (synthesis model, effect scale).
  6. S4 = Result (lead estimate + confidence interval).
  7. S5 = Robustness (heterogeneity, sensitivity).
  8. S6 = Interpretation (plain-language meaning).
  9. S7 = Boundary (main limitation).
