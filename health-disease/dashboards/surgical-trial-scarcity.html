Why It Matters

The Evidence   129 words · target 156
Does clinical trial investment in Surgery across SAARC nations match regional need, given that south asia faces a surgical workforce crisis with 0.7 surgeons per 100? This cross-sectional audit examined 567 Surgery trials across 8 SAARC nations on ClinicalTrials.gov. The primary estimand was the rate ratio comparing per-capita trial density, computed from registry-level metadata. SAARC hosted 567 Surgery trials with India accounting for 49%, yielding a 5.7-fold per-capita disparity. Sensitivity analyses using alternative inequality metrics confirmed the primary finding with consistent direction and magnitude.
