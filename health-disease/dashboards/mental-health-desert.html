Does clinical trial investment in Depression across SAARC nations match regional need, given that south asia has 0.3 psychiatrists per 100? This cross-sectional audit examined 458 Depression trials across 8 SAARC nations on ClinicalTrials.gov. The primary estimand was the Shannon entropy of trial allocation, computed from registry-level metadata. SAARC hosted 458 Depression trials with India accounting for 46%, yielding a 6.0-fold per-capita disparity.
